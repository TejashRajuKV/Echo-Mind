About Echo Mind

Echo Mind represents the cutting edge of AI-powered fact-checking technology by Thunderwing Falcons. Our prototype combines the power of Google's Vertex AI platform with Gemini 2.5 Pro models and BigQuery database to provide accurate, educational, and engaging fact-checking experiences.

Built for the modern information age, Echo Mind not only identifies misinformation but also educates users on how to become better critical thinkers through gamified learning and personalized insights.
